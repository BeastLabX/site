BeastLabX App

Features

The BeastLabX App is built around focused tools that support serious training without unnecessary clutter.

🏋️

Structured Programs

Follow clear training programs built around structure, consistency, and progression.

BeastLabX is designed to help lifters stay organized instead of jumping between random workouts.

📋

Workout Tracking

Log sets, reps, and weight during your sessions in a layout built for serious lifting.

The focus stays on getting through the session cleanly and reviewing what was actually done.

⏱️

HIIT Mode

Run interval sessions with a simple active and rest timer designed to stay fast and easy to use.

HIIT is included as a focused training tool, not as a gimmick layered into the rest of the app.

📚

Exercise Library

Train with a large library of strength and bodybuilding exercises.

The app is built to support real gym training with movement variety instead of a minimal starter list.

📈

Progress Stats

Review training history and track performance over time.

BeastLabX keeps the emphasis on useful progression data rather than turning stats into noise.

🟩

Share Results

Generate clean workout result cards that present the achievement clearly.

The goal is to let the result stand out, not bury it under flashy effects or clutter.
