Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Introduction

BeastLabX respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the BeastLabX website and applications.

Information Collected

BeastLabX applications are designed to operate entirely on your device. Training data such as workouts, exercises, and statistics are stored locally on your device.

BeastLabX does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

Use of Information

Any data used within the application is solely for providing functionality such as tracking workouts, displaying training history, and calculating statistics.

BeastLabX does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

BeastLabX may display advertisements using third-party services such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect non-personal data such as device information and interaction data to improve ad performance and relevance.

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data Security

BeastLabX is designed with a privacy-first approach. All user data remains stored locally on the user’s device and is not transmitted externally.

Children’s Privacy

BeastLabX is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the application or legal requirements.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the BeastLabX website contact page.
