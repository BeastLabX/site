Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Introduction

BeastLabX respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the BeastLabX website and applications.

Information Collected

BeastLabX applications are designed to operate primarily on your device. Training data such as workouts, exercises, and statistics are stored locally on your device unless otherwise stated.

Some features may interact with third-party services such as Android Health Connect in order to read workout-related metrics like heart rate or calories burned. BeastLabX does not control how these third-party services manage their data.

Use of Information

Any data collected through the application is used only for the purpose of providing functionality within the app, such as tracking workouts, displaying training history, and calculating statistics.

BeastLabX does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Some features may rely on third-party services such as:

• Android Health Connect for workout metrics
• Platform services provided by the Android operating system

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data Security

BeastLabX is designed with a privacy-first approach. Most user data remains stored locally on the user’s device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the application or legal requirements.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the BeastLabX website contact page.
