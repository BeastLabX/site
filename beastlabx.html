What BeastLabX App is built to do

BeastLabX App is designed to support structured execution, real workout logging, and long-term progression for serious lifters.

Structured Programs

Follow organized training programs built for consistency and progression instead of random workouts.

Workout Tracking

Log sets, reps, and weight quickly during training sessions with a layout designed for real lifting.

HIIT Mode

Run interval sessions using a clean active and rest timer designed specifically for HIIT workouts.

Exercise Library

Train with a large library of strength and bodybuilding exercises covering real gym training.

Progress Stats

Review training history and track performance over time to support long-term progression.

Share Results

Generate clean workout result cards designed to highlight the achievement.

Planned Direction

BeastLabX is being built with long-term progression in mind. Future development is expected to expand training insights, program tools, wearable support, and export options while keeping the app focused and practical.

Deeper Training Insights

Expanded statistics and clearer training summaries to make long-term progression easier to review.

Expanded Program Tools

More capable planning and program-building features designed to support structured training over time.

Better Wearable Support

Improved device compatibility and stronger integration support for users who train with wearable data.

Export and Print Options

Cleaner export tools for sharing results, printing programs, and keeping training records accessible.
