ABOUT BEASTLABX

Built for real-world apps.

BeastLabX builds practical, high-performance applications designed for real-world use. Every app is focused, efficient, and built to solve actual problems — not add unnecessary complexity.

What We Build

BeastLabX develops a range of focused applications across different industries:

• Fitness systems for structured training and progression
• Workshop tools for diagnostics, job tracking, and repairs
• Utility apps designed for speed and everyday use

Our Approach

We don’t build bloated apps. Every product is designed to be fast, clear, and purpose-driven.

No unnecessary features. No distractions. Just tools that work.

The Goal

To create a strong ecosystem of practical apps under the BeastLabX brand — each one solving a real problem, and doing it properly.

The Creator

BeastLabX was created by Niel Coetzee with a focus on building tools that he actually wanted to use — practical, efficient, and built with real-world use in mind.
