Why BeastLabX Exists

Most fitness apps try to do everything at once — social feeds, endless features, and complicated interfaces.

But serious lifters do not need more noise. They need a training log that helps them follow structured programs, record workouts properly, and track real progress over time.

BeastLabX exists to be that tool: a serious training log built around discipline, structure, and long-term progression.

The Creator

BeastLabX was created by Niel Coetzee, a lifter and developer who wanted a serious training log without unnecessary complexity.

After trying many fitness apps that focused on gimmicks instead of progression, he decided to build the tracker he actually wanted to use himself.

— Niel Coetzee
Creator of BeastLabX
