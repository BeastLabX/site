FAQ

Answers across all BeastLabX apps.

Common questions about BeastLabX, including all apps in the ecosystem.

What is BeastLabX?

BeastLabX builds practical, high-performance apps across fitness and real-world tools.

What apps are available?

BeastLabX (training log), MechToolbox (mechanic tools), BeastUtility (gym tools), and BeastBlueprint (program builder).

Which app should I use?

Use BeastLabX for training, MechToolbox for workshop work, BeastUtility for quick gym tools, and BeastBlueprint for program creation.

Where can I download the apps?

Available apps can be downloaded from the Google Play Store. Others will show “Coming Soon” until release.

Why are some apps marked “Coming Soon”?

Some apps are still being built and tested to ensure they meet quality standards before release.

Do I need an account?

No. Apps are designed to work without accounts in v1 for simplicity and speed.

Do the apps work offline?

Yes. Core functionality is designed to work without an internet connection.

Is BeastLabX available on iOS?

Currently Android only. iOS may be considered in future versions.

How do I report a bug or get support?

Use the Contact page or reach out through the official BeastLabX channels.

Will my data be saved long-term?

Yes. Apps like MechToolbox are designed to keep records available long-term on your device.
