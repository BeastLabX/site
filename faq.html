FAQ

Common questions about the BeastLabX ecosystem.

What is BeastLabX?

BeastLabX is a training ecosystem focused on serious strength and bodybuilding training. The flagship app is designed as a structured workout log for lifters who want clarity and consistency in their training.

Is BeastLabX free?

Yes. The BeastLabX App is planned to be free to use. Future optional features may expand the ecosystem as the platform grows.

What apps are part of the BeastLabX ecosystem?

The ecosystem currently includes the BeastLabX App and BeastBlueprint, a structured workout program builder. Additional tools are planned for the future.

When will the BeastLabX App launch?

The BeastLabX App is currently in development and testing. Updates about availability will be announced through the website.

Who is BeastLabX built for?

BeastLabX is built for lifters who take their training seriously. The focus is on structured workouts, progression, and long-term consistency instead of casual fitness tracking.
