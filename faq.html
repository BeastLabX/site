Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about BeastLabX and the BeastLabX ecosystem.

What is BeastLabX?

BeastLabX is a serious training log designed for lifters who want structured programs, clear tracking, and long-term progression without unnecessary features.

When will the BeastLabX app launch?

The app is currently in development and testing. Launch is planned soon.

Will BeastLabX support wearables?

Yes. BeastLabX integrates with Android Health Connect for supported wearable data.

Will more tools be added?

Yes. BeastLabX is being built as an ecosystem with additional tools like BeastBlueprint.
